About LuxVision

Capturing Luxury Properties Across Mauritius

LuxVision Photography specializes in creating stunning visual content for luxury real estate properties throughout Mauritius. With an eye for detail and composition, we transform ordinary spaces into extraordinary visual experiences.

Our approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to showcase properties in their best light, helping real estate agents and property owners attract discerning clients and achieve premium sales and rental prices.

Whether you're marketing a beachfront villa, a luxury penthouse, or an exclusive estate, our photography will highlight the unique features and atmosphere that make your property special.

Our Services

Rental Property Photography

Specialized photography packages designed to showcase your property for short-term rental platforms like Airbnb and Booking.com. Our images highlight the features that travelers are looking for, helping you stand out in a competitive market.

  • Interior and exterior shots
  • Amenity highlights
  • Lifestyle and atmosphere images
  • Quick turnaround for faster listing

Sales Property Photography

Comprehensive photography services for properties on the market. We capture the architectural details, spatial relationships, and unique selling points that help potential buyers envision themselves in the space.

  • Architectural detail shots
  • Wide-angle room perspectives
  • Feature highlighting
  • Twilight/dusk photography

Aerial & Drone Photography

Elevate your property marketing with stunning aerial perspectives that showcase the property's location, surroundings, and scale. Perfect for larger estates, beachfront properties, and highlighting proximity to amenities.

  • Property overview shots
  • Location context
  • Surrounding landscape
  • Unique aerial perspectives

Property Video Tours

Immersive video walkthroughs that give potential buyers or renters a realistic sense of moving through the property. Our cinematic approach creates an emotional connection to the space.

  • Smooth cinematic movements
  • Natural lighting emphasis
  • Highlight key selling features
  • Optional narration or music
